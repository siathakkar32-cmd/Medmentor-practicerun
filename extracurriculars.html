Your Starter EC Strategy

For a pre-med focused applicant, the goal is not to do random clubs. The goal is to build a clear story: healthcare interest, service, leadership, research or learning, and real community impact.

  1. Start with service: volunteer at a hospital, clinic, nursing home, blood drive, or health nonprofit.
  2. Add leadership: become an officer, start a chapter, organize a health event, or lead a fundraiser.
  3. Create impact: build something measurable like a health workshop, awareness campaign, tutoring program, or AI health education tool.
  4. Go deeper: add research, science competitions, certifications, or a summer program when possible.
  5. Track everything: record hours, responsibilities, results, awards, and personal reflections.
